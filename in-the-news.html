Africa/Japan Startups Pitch - Innovation & New Partnerships for the Sustainable Development Goals

August, 2019

"On August 30, on the last day of the 7th Tokyo International Conference on African Development (TICAD7), a startup pitch event was held by JETRO, JICA and UNDP. About 30 startups aiming to solve social issues in Africa gathered on the pitch. The PwC Award was awarded to Health Direct in Ghana. In Ghana, many people have died because of the lack of medical infrastructure and lack of medical information, without adequate medical care. Health Direct helps patients improve their access to healthcare through an on-demand platform that connects patients with healthcare providers. By creating an individual's medical ID that includes their own history and connecting them to medical personnel on the platform, patients can introduce and make appropriate medical institutions, make appointments, and receive prescriptions and medications at the nearest institution in the event of an emergency....."
