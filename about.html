Financial Inclusion for Healthcare

In emerging markets across the world, the middle class is booming and so is their need for quality, affordable health care that matches their budget. With mobile-phone penetration at an all-time high, Health Direct provides a Unique opportunity to deliver alternative health care financing through a blockchain-based payments infrastructure that improves health care outcomes for patients and reduces burden for health service providers.
