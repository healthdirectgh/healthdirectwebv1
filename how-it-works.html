Healthcare at the speed of life

We design our products to serve the needs of patients and health service providers because we believe every second counts.

A commitment to improved Healthcare outcomes

At Health Direct Global, everything we do is focused on increasing access to healthcare, reducing costs for patients and improving care and patient outcomes through a network of health service providers.

Making a live-saving decision

With the explicit permission of our customers, we analyze data to determine financial eligibility. Our machine learning algorithms process diverse data points to create personalized alternative health care financing options through a blockchain-based payments infrastructure in a transparent manner that reduces the shock caused by emergency out of pocket payments at the point of care.
